Recruitment Information:



We encourage all to apply regardless of skill level, since we believe in training our recruits and investing in them. We want to see you develop in your interests and abilities here at Sparkstone. Our most successful members all exhibit a passion for their part of the project and we will give you a place to develop that interest.

We have provided these roles to help applicants better understand what they might be doing, but, by no means are our members constrained to the expectations of the role. We provide inward mobility as we have the flexibility of a smaller organization.

If you have more questions about these roles, do not hesitate to reach out to contact@sparkstoneanalytics.com.
